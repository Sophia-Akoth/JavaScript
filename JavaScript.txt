Algorithm
1.Check if you have enough airtime by dialling *144#
2.Recharge airtime if it is not enough.
3.Dial *544# or *444# and press the call button.
4.Select the option "buy bundles"
5.Choose the bundle option number and select "send"
6.Select whether to pay via airtime or Mpesa and click to send.
7.A confirmation message will pop-up select "accept" and send.
